What is Webbetto?

We noticed something simple: many great local businesses still have no website. That means customers can’t easily find them, trust them, or contact them. Webbetto exists to fix that — quickly, professionally, and without expensive agency costs.

We start with websites because they’re the foundation. Once Webbetto grows, our bigger goal is to create new software products that help businesses work smarter — from simple tools to full applications.

Our Story — Omar & Alessandro

Webbetto was born from a long-standing friendship. Omar and Alessandro have been friends since high school in Italy. Even as life took them in different directions, their connection never faded.

Alessandro began working in the United States, while Omar built his career in Vienna. Both work in the IT industry, and both share the same ambition: to become independent and create software that makes a real difference.

Over the years, they traveled to many places together, exchanged ideas, and kept coming back to the same question: what if we built something of our own? Webbetto is the first step — starting with affordable, modern websites for local businesses, and growing toward bigger software products in the future.

Our promise: keep it simple, keep it honest, and deliver something you’re proud to share.
